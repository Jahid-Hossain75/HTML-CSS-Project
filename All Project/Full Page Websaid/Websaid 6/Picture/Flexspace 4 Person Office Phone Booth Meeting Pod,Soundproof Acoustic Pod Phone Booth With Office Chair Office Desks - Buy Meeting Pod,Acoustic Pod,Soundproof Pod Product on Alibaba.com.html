    About Pods

    Q: Can I customize color for my pods?
    A: Of course. Color can be customized as per RAL color chart. Please feel free to provide required color number and pod quantity to our sales. We will quote accordingly.

    Q: How about size? Is it customizable?
    A: Yes. Flexspace's pods are highly modular which benefits for customization. Not only color, but also size and function. Our design team will offer solution as per your demands.

    Q: Is there MOQ?
    A: There's no MOQ for standard pod. MOQ will be required for customized pods. Feel free to ask us for exact rules.

    About Delivery

    Q: What's the production time?
    A: It depends on your order quantity and stock. If your order are in stock, one week is enough. If not, it'll take about two to four weeks.

    Q: How many can be uploaded in one container?
    A: Please check "product package" part, all package details are shared there.

    Q: What're transportation terms?
    A: EXW, FOB, CIF, DDU and DDP are all available.

    About Payment

    Q: What's the payment term?
    A: 30% deposit before production, 70% balance before shipping.

    Q: Is there any discount as per order quantity?
    A: Sure. Feel free to consult with us for details.
